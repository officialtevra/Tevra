Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link will be valid for 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking from most Indian banks.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most digital products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our support team within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund. Some products may have different return policies which will be clearly stated at the time of purchase.

Do you offer technical support for your products?

Yes, we offer free technical support for all our products. You can contact our support team via email, phone, or through the contact form on this page. Our typical response time is under 2 hours during business hours.
